Resumen

En un futuro oscuro, la Tierra ha sido devastada por el consumismo. Gaia, debilitada pero decidida, despierta para restaurar la armonía en un planeta al borde del colapso. Con cada batalla, recuperarás poder y enfrentarás a los enemigos que buscan la destrucción total de la naturaleza.

Frequently Asked Questions

Frequently asked questions, here are the most common questions asked by our visitors.

What is the purpose of the game?

To teach the importance of preserving the environment using an entertainment medium.

What do the towers represent?

The towers represent each known biological kingdom “Animal, Plant, Fungi, Protoctista and Monera”.

In what environment was the video game developed?

In Unity 2D.

In what environment did you develop the PWA site?

In the visual studio IDE, using .NET, Blazor WebAssembly and Bootstrap for the styles.

What do the enemies represent?

The enemies represent environmental contaminants, such as, in general, the burning of fuels and garbage.
